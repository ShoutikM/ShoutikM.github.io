Biography

I'm currently a Postdoctoral Scholar in the Glaser Lab at Northwestern University. I received my Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering at the University of Maryland working under the supervision of Prof. Behtash Babadi and Prof. Shihab Shamma. My M.S. in Electrical Engineering and B.S. in Electrical Engineering with Honors were also completed the University of Maryland. My research interests are in modeling how the brain processes stimuli to generate behavior through the development and application of system- and network-level analyses of high-dimensional neuronal recordings.
